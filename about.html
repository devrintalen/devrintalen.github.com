Hi, I'm Devrin Talen and I like to code, solder, and eat my wife's amazing cooking.

In my spare time I tinker with a worn STK500, write posts for this blog, and enjoy life in Boston with my wonderful wife and our little furball of a dog. I live in an Emacs world; a mix of org-mode, c-mode and gnus (with a little help from BBDB). I like to write in LaTeX. I hack on a Fujitsu Lifebook (one of the dubiously-constructed laptops of the Windows Tablet era) that runs a very nicely configured Arch Linux installation.

Feel free to get in touch with me at dct23@cornell.edu. You can also get a copy of my resume.
