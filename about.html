Hi, I'm Devrin Talen and I like to code, solder, and eat my wife's amazing cooking.

In my spare time I tinker with a worn STK500, write posts for this blog, and enjoy life in Boston with my wonderful wife and our little furball of a dog. I live in an Emacs world; a mix of org-mode, c-mode and gnus (with a little help from BBDB). I like to write in LaTeX. I hack on an Apple Macbook that runs a very nicely configured Ubuntu installation.

Feel free to get in touch with me at me@devrintalen.net. You can also get a copy of my resume.
